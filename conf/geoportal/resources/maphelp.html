Navigating In the Map

Pan: Left-Click (Hold) and Drag

Zoom: Use the [+] or [-] zoom buttons OR mouse scroll wheel

ZoomToBox: Shift+Left-Click (Hold) and Drag Box

Changing Layers: Expand the “Map Layer Selection” panel and select the desired layer/s.

Selecting a Frame

Set the “Select System” value in the Menu then single click anywhere on the map.

Measuring

Distance: Select ‘measure distance’ and single click to draw a line

Area: Select ‘measure area and single click to draw a polygon. Double click to end drawing.

Drawing

Draw: To draw a polygon (sets a download extent) select “Draw a Polygon”. Single click to add vertices and double click to end the drawing.

Clear or Re-Draw: Select “Clear Drawing” or select “Draw a Polygon” to clear and draw a new area.

Changing Locations

Select the tab at the top of the portal to change areas.
